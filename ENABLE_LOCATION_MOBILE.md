## The Issue
You're seeing "Tracking disabled" because the browser doesn't have permission to access your location.

## Quick Fix - 3 Steps

### Step 1: Click "Enable Tracking" Button
1. On the Tourist App page
2. Scroll down to the bottom
3. Click the **"Enable Tracking"** button (orange/red button)
4. A popup will appear asking for location permission

### Step 2: Allow Location Access
When the browser asks "Allow location access?":
- Click **"Allow"** or **"Yes"**
- Do NOT click "Block" or "Deny"

### Step 3: Wait for Location
- You'll see "Getting your location..."
- After a few seconds, your real location will appear
- The button will turn green and say "Tracking On"

---

## If It Still Doesn't Work

### For Android Chrome:

1. **Open Chrome Settings:**
   - Tap the 3 dots (‚ãÆ) in top-right
   - Go to **Settings**

2. **Site Settings:**
   - Tap **Site settings**
   - Tap **Location**

3. **Allow Location:**
   - Make sure Location is **ON**
   - Find your site in the list
   - Tap it and select **Allow**

4. **Refresh the page** and click "Enable Tracking" again

### For iPhone Safari:

1. **Open iPhone Settings:**
   - Go to **Settings** app
   - Scroll down to **Safari**

2. **Location Settings:**
   - Tap **Location**
   - Select **"While Using the App"** or **"Ask"**

3. **Privacy Settings:**
   - Go back to main Settings
   - Tap **Privacy & Security**
   - Tap **Location Services**
   - Make sure it's **ON**
   - Scroll down to **Safari**
   - Select **"While Using"**

4. **Refresh the page** and click "Enable Tracking" again

## Troubleshooting

### "Location access denied"
- You clicked "Block" or "Deny"
- Follow the steps above to allow it in settings

### "Location unavailable"
- GPS might be off on your phone
- Go to phone Settings ‚Üí Location ‚Üí Turn ON

### "GPS not available"
- Your device doesn't support GPS
- Try on a different device

### Still not working?
1. Close the browser completely
2. Reopen it
3. Go to the Tourist App page
4. Click "Enable Tracking"
5. Allow location when asked
